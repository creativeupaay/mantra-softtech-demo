### Behavioral Biometrics Technology Key Aspects

Behavioral biometrics technology identifies individuals based on unique behavioral patterns, like keystroke dynamics, gait, and voice recognition. This dynamic, non-intrusive method offers continuous authentication, enhancing security in areas like online banking and device access. Its complex, changeable nature makes it hard to mimic, providing a sophisticated layer of protection while maintaining user convenience and addressing evolving security challenges.

### AI & ML in Enhancing Biometric Systems

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) significantly enhance the capabilities of biometric systems, revolutionizing their accuracy and efficiency. At the core, AI algorithms interpret biometric data with greater precision, reducing false positives and negatives. This improvement is crucial in applications like identity verification, where accuracy is paramount. ML, on the other hand, continually improves system performance. By learning from new biometric data, ML algorithms adapt and refine their pattern recognition capabilities.
