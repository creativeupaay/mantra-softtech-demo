[**Embedded Software Engineering** \\
Embedded Software Engineering is the method of controlling diverse devices and machines. Integrating software program with non computer devices leads to the formation of embedded systems. A typical embedded system requires many programming tools, microprocessors, and operating systems, created by embedded software engineers, who tailor their development of the hardware it will control and on which it will run.\\
