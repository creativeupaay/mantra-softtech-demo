## Mantra Softech a Global Leaders in Biometric Technology and India’s Largest Biometric Manufacturer

Mantra Softech is a global hi tech manufacturer of biometric products and solutions. We offer a wide range of products in biometric and RFID industry. This dream project of like minded visionaries started in 2006 continues to push the envelope on biometric system capabilities. Customer's rapidly growing requirements for complex and sophisticated security system are the inspiration behind Mantra's Innovative products and solutions.

Globally recognized as one of the leaders in this field, Mantra continues to position itself as an innovation driven brand. Mantra's product portfolio includes Fingerprint Sensors, IRIS Sensor, Integrated Fingerprint Devices, Integrated IRIS Devices, POS / MicroATM / Financial Terminal, IRIS Recognition in Mobile and many more biometric devices. New products and solutions are added every year in our services and in the product category to meet the future needs of business in terms of their security concerns. Our security solutions include Business Security Systems, Enterprise Security Solution, Integrated Security Solution, Access control system, Smart City Solution, Airport Solution, suitable for application in both SME and large enterprises.

## Why Mantra is a Global Leader in Biometric Technology

### Pioneer in National  Identity Systems

Mantra played a foundational role in India’s Aadhaar program—the world’s largest biometric identity system—by supplying government-approved fingerprint and IRIS devices. Our Aadhaar-enabled products power secure authentication for millions daily.

### End-to-End Biometric  Manufacturing

With a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Ahmedabad, Mantra designs, develops, and manufactures a full range of biometric devices in-house. This allows for tighter quality control, rapid innovation, and seamless scalability.

### Strong R&D  Backbone

Our dedicated in-house R&D team constantly innovates to develop next-gen biometric solutions—from AI-powered facial recognition to mobile IRIS scanning. Mantra’s technology roadmap is built on future-proofing digital identity.

### Global Reach with  Local Expertise

With a strong presence across India and export operations in Africa, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia, Mantra combines local market knowledge with global product standards—delivering solutions tailored to each region.

### Trusted by Government  & Enterprise

Mantra serves:

80% of central government offices in India.

20% of India’s top 50 corporates Our solutions are used in sectors ranging from banking and telecom to smart cities, public distribution, and defense.

### Largest Biometric  Manufacturer in India

With over 1400 employees, 8 branch offices, and support teams in 24 states, Mantra has the operational scale to deliver nationwide deployment and 24/7 service.

### Innovation-Driven  Product Portfolio

From integrated biometric devices to secure financial terminals and enterprise access control systems, Mantra’s evolving product suite addresses real-world identity and security challenges.

### Commitment to  Quality & Compliance

Mantra follows stringent ISO standards and is STQC, UIDAI, and iBeta certified—ensuring all products meet the highest global compliance benchmarks.
