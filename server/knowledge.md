## Glossary of Terms

### AEBAS
**Aadhaar Enabled Biometric Attendance System** - Biometric attendance systems that integrate with India's Aadhaar identification system

### Aadhaar
India's biometric-based digital identity system used for citizen identification and authentication

### FAP
**Fingerprint Acquisition Profile** - Standards for fingerprint scanner certification (FAP20, FAP30, etc.)

### OEM
**Original Equipment Manufacturer** - Companies that manufacture products or components that are purchased by another company and retailed under that purchasing company's brand name

### ODM
**Original Design Manufacturer** - Company that designs and manufactures a product as specified and eventually rebranded by another firm for sale

### eKYC
**Electronic Know Your Customer** - Digital identity verification process using biometric authentication

### STQC
**Standard Testing and Quality Certification** - Government of India certification for IT products

### CMMI
**Capability Maturity Model Integration** - Process level improvement training and appraisal program

### RFID
**Radio-Frequency Identification** - Technology using electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects

### VSaaS
**Video Surveillance as a Service** - Cloud-based video surveillance solution
